How to quote?

Please visit the request a quote page here then fill out the form provided by filling in your first name, last name, telephone number, and email. Then you will be contacted shortly by our Project Manager or you can reach out our hotline email support at admin@translationtransfer.com.

How to be a freelance translator at Translation Transfer?

Please visit the career page here And then click on Freelance Opportunity button. By clicking the button, you will have to fill out the form provided accordingly and responsibly. Finally, you only need to wait for our HR team to contact you.

Does Translation Transfer enable to handle project on the weekend?

Yes, it does. Our company is still online and available for any projects to be handled on the weekend. However, we are not available to handle projects during the Indonesian National Holidays. Yet, you can still request a quote during our holiday. We would love to keep in touch with you to answer your queries.

Does Translation Transfer enable to use CAT Tool?

Yes, it does. Our company has been dealing with various projects from various consumers using CAT Tools. All of consumers certainly have particular CAT Tools that fit their projects well so we will serve you with the CAT Tools upon the request. Simply tell us what CAT Tools fit your project best.

I am a local client in Indonesia. Does Translation Transfer enable to handle local project?

Yes, we can kindly assist you to handle your project. For Indonesian clients, we would like to generously direct you to our terms and conditions. Within our terms and conditions, you will have the information related to the price and other relevant information.
